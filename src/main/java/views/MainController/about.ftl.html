About us

We are team of students who faced the same problem as the regular high school graduate: Which university to apply? Searching the requirements, scholarship opportunities, deadlines of each university is a hard and wearying process. We offer a platform where students can find suitable universities according to their SAT, ORT, TOEFL, IELTS scores. At the same time we encourage to achieve higher goals.
